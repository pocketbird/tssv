The Still, Small Voice

The Still, Small Voice is melodic indie-rock based out of Nashville, TN.

Originating as the solo-project of Christiana Benton, In Tension (2013) and the Mourning Dove EP (2014) were both made possible through the generosity of friends, family, and strangers, and are available for free download via Noisetrade.

This year brings MANAGE, a new concept EP about control, surrender, and the questioning of ingrained beliefs.


To be released early fall 2016.

Be sure to check out their tour dates and follow TSSV on social media to stay in the loop.
