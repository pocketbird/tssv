The Still, Small Voice

The Still, Small Voice is melodic indie-rock based out of Nashville, TN. Originating as the solo-project of Christiana Benton, In Tension (2013) and the Mourning Dove EP (2014) were both made possible through the generosity of friends, family, and strangers, and are available for free download via Noisetrade.

2015 holds a lot of potential as the band embarks on multiple tours while polishing songs toward a new album to be released in early spring, 2016. Overall, The Still, Small Voice's motive is to create an atmosphere of honesty and connectedness among their listeners. It's music to feel your feelings to - just enough positive mixed in with the melancholy.
